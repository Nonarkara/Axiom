Aerial Intelligence

Eyes in the sky.
Intelligence on the ground.

Our dashboards monitor cities from above. Our drone fleet takes it further — from satellite overview to street-level precision. AI turns both into action.

Survey & Mapping

See what satellites can't

We combine satellite imagery with low-altitude drone surveys to create centimeter-accurate 3D maps, orthomosaics, and terrain models. Hidden infrastructure damage, crop stress, flood risk zones — our drones find what orbital sensors miss.

Agriculture

Precision farming at scale

AI-powered navigation systems for crop monitoring, targeted spraying, and yield prediction. We design flight paths that optimize coverage while minimizing resource waste.

Delivery

Last-mile, reimagined

Navigation systems for autonomous delivery in dense urban corridors and remote rural terrain. Real-time routing that accounts for wind, obstacles, and no-fly zones.

Resilience & Relief

First responders in the air

When disaster strikes, our drones deploy within minutes for damage assessment, survivor detection, and supply drops. AI analytics determine optimal resource mobilization in real time.

Dashboard Integration

Seamless with our systems

Every drone feed plugs directly into our existing city dashboards. No separate platform. Aerial data flows into the same interface your team already uses — real-time, unified, zero friction.

AI Analytics

Real-time intelligence, not raw footage

Every drone in our fleet feeds live data into our AI engine. Object detection, change analysis, thermal mapping, NDVI vegetation indices — processed on the edge, delivered as actionable insight. We don't give you video. We give you answers.

The Launch

SLIC Index launched on the
world's smartest stage.

174 cities
53 countries
3,000+ professionals
2,250 booths

Dr. Non Arkara was one of two keynote speakers at the City Vision stage — the centerpiece of the Smart City Summit & Expo (SCSE) 2026 in Taipei. The Vice President of Taiwan opened the event. The Mayor of Taipei spoke on stage. Foreign ministers and city leaders from around the world gathered under one roof.

He showed them a war dashboard built in 45 minutes. A bus tracker that runs without GPS. A citizen reporting system that cut response times from 67 hours to 2. Then he launched the SLIC Index. 103 cities. Five dimensions. Kaohsiung: #1. Taipei: #2. Bangkok: #4. Singapore: #8.

The room went quiet. Then the questions started. A European mayor's alliance asked to use it instead of The Economist's index. City leaders from across Asia and the Americas came up afterward: “Can you do this for my city?”

“We built an index. You build the ranking.”

Non & Poon — the two behind Axiom

Co-Founder

Dr. Non Arkaraprasertkul

PhD Anthropology, Harvard MPhil Chinese Studies, Oxford MSc Architecture, MIT

Architect, urban anthropologist, and builder. Trained across Harvard, Oxford, and MIT — then spent years living in Shanghai's alleyways researching gentrification and urban social change. Former Visiting Lecturer at MIT, Distinguished Visiting Professor at University of South Florida, postdoctoral fellow at NYU. Published in Urban Studies, Asian Anthropology, and cited over 400 times. Built production-grade AI dashboards from a single laptop. Designs systems grounded in ethnographic observation — because understanding people is the precondition for building things that work.

Co-Founder

Dr. Poon Thiengburanathum

PhD Civil Engineering Assoc. Prof., Chiang Mai University School of Engineering School of Public Policy

Civil engineer, smart city strategist, and operational anchor. Associate Professor at Chiang Mai University's School of Engineering, also affiliated with the School of Public Policy. Drafted Chiang Mai's Smart City Master Plan (2018–2023). Researches cities as complex adaptive systems — real-time bus prediction, decision support for public transport, sustainable infrastructure. The one who keeps the engine running while ambition keeps scaling.
